Introduction to Jaamacadda Ummadda

What do you know about Jaamacadda Ummadda?

Jaamacadda Ummadda Soomaaliyeed is one of the largest universities in Somalia, located in Mogadishu,
the capital city of the country. It was founded in 1988 and is dedicated to advancing knowledge and research both locally and internationally.

Key Facts About Jaamacadda Ummadda Soomaaliyeed:

  1. History:

    Jaamacadda Ummadda Soomaaliyeed was established with the main objective of enhancing knowledge and research in the country. It also contributes to the development of the society and economy of Somalia.

  2. Faculties at Jaamacadda Ummadda:

    Here are some key faculties at Jaamacadda Ummadda Soomaaliyeed:

    • Faculty of Medicine
    • Faculty of Engineering
    • Faculty of Social Sciences
    • Faculty of Languages
    • Faculty of Science
    • Faculty of Education
    • Faculty of Economics
    • Faculty of Sharia
    • Faculty of Law
  3. Degrees Offered:

    Jaamacadda Ummadda Soomaaliyeed (JUS) offers various degrees and educational programs. The university offers a range of degrees to students looking to acquire knowledge and skills:

    • Bachelor’s Degree (First Cycle)
    • Master’s Degree (Second Cycle)
    • PhD (Doctoral Degree)
    • Diplomas and Certification Programs
  4. University Structure:

    Jaamacadda Ummadda Soomaaliyeed (JUS) has a strong organizational structure that adapts to the educational and research needs of the Somali community. It is organized into different departments and offices that work together to ensure quality education.

  5. Students and Youth:

    Students and youth are among the most important groups that Jaamacadda Ummadda Soomaaliyeed (JUS) serves. The university specifically addresses the needs and aspirations of students and youth, offering opportunities for education, personal growth, and skill development.

  6. Reconstruction and Development:

    Reconstruction and development are key areas that Jaamacadda Ummadda Soomaaliyeed (JUS) is involved in to support the Somali community, especially in the fields of education, research, and skills development. The university contributes to rebuilding and advancing the country.
